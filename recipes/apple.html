Apple cake recipe

A delightful combination of tender, cinnamon-spiced apple slices folded into
a moist and fluffy cake batter, creating a heavenly dessert that captures the essence of autumn

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Butter or vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to a specified temperature, typically around 350°F (175°C), and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon. Mix well.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter or oil with the sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, followed by the milk and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Gently fold in the thinly sliced apples, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  7. Pour the batter into the greased baking pan, spreading it out evenly.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for the specified time, typically around 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Optionally, dust the cooled apple cake with powdered sugar, or serve it as is for a delicious treat.
